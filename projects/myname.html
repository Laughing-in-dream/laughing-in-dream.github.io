My name

  • 文恺(Wenkai) 王(Wang): My name is Wenkai Wang, with "Wang" being my family name and "Wenkai" my given name. "Wen" in my name symbolizes extensive knowledge, while "恺(Kai)" represents happiness. It's interesting to note that many Chinese names with "Kai" are written as "凯," which means victory. Throughout my life, many people have misspelled my name, often mistaking it for the more common meaning. My parents chose this name for me to convey a philosophy: the importance of happiness and joy is over victory. Luckily, they not only believed in this but also taught me in this way.

  • 做梦笑醒(laughing_in_dream): My online nickname is "Laughing in Dream" or "做梦笑醒" (zuo meng xiao xing), which translates to waking up from a dream with laughter. This name reflects my love for sleeping and the dreams are always vivid, colorful. I remember these dreams clearly after waking up. The nickname signifies my hope to find joy even in my dreams, highlighting my love for the happy and surreal world of dreaming.
